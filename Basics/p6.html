Defining color value

Colors in CSS most often specifies in the following formates:

  1. A color keyword - like - "red" , "green" "blue" , "tranparent" , etc .
  2. A HEX value - "#ff0000" , "#00ff00" , etc
  3. An RGB values (RED , GREEN , BLUE )-like "rgb(255,0,0)"
